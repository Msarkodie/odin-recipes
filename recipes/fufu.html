Fufu

Description

This food is very delicious and eaten in mostly West Africa even though it is now crossing boundaries. Ghanaians are mostly attributed to this kind of food This i just for your eyes.

I will show you how to prepare just in case you are already salivating.

Ingredients

  • Casava
  • Plantain
  • Already prepared soup (I am feeling lazy.Already prepared soup would do. Yeah any kind.)
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Peel the casava and Plantain
  2. Slice them into small pieces or sizes and put them on fire
  3. When cooked, get your mortar and pestle(Dont ask me what those are. Just find out for yourself)
  4. Pound the cassava and plantain in the mortar using the pestle until the desired shape and texture is attained
  5. Put into a bowl and add your soup and meat. Well mine is ready. Byeeeee.
