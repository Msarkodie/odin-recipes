Fufu

Description

This food is very delicious and in Ghana here it is referred to as a cotinental dish. Well it is not what you are thinking. Well I understand that the name may seem as a dog meat but its not. .

I will show you how to prepare just in case you are already salivating.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sausage meat
  • Vegetables
  • White Cream

Steps

  1. Bake the bread or buy one from a store. Well, how to bake bread is not part of my business now.
  2. Wrap the bread around the sausage meat. See, you have to wrap it well. Dont say I didnt tell you.
  3. Now, get the vegetables to fill the inside of the bread. This could be arranged nicely.
  4. Pour the cream on the whole structure now and slightly bake it
  5. You can now enjoy your hotdog. Not hot dog though
