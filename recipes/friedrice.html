Fried Rice

Description

This food is very delicious and eaten in all the continent there are. It is a fast food also. This is just for your eyes

I will show you how to prepare just in case you are already salivating.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Vegetables
  • Slices of meat

Steps

  1. Put Oil into Saucepan and allow it to fry
  2. Slice your vegetables and put into oil
  3. After some time add your sliced meat
  4. Then you add your already cooked rice in about 15 minutes
  5. Stir or turn the food until evenly cooked and mixed
