Jollof

Description

This food is very delicious and eaten in mostly West Africa even though it is now crossing boundaries. Very popular are the Ghanaian and Nigerian jollof. Currently there is a conflict between both countries about who's is best

I will show you how to prepare just in case you are already salivating.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato stew (Dont frown ok, you can google how to prepare if you want)
  • Vegetables
  • Fried chicken
  • Cooked beans (I like mine with beans)

Steps

  1. Put the stew on fire for it to stimmer and add the vegetable to give flavour and colour
  2. After some time, please add a measurable amount of water depending on how many cups of rice you are cooking
  3. Allow to cook for about 15 minutes and pour your cups of rice into it and allow to cook
  4. Occasionally, stir and add water until evenly cooked. Becareful as there is this tendency that the food wont be evenly cooked
  5. Now serve yourself and add the fried chicken. Hey come here dont go yet. Oh, he is gone.
